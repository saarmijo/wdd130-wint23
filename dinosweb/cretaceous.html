Cretaceous

The Cretaceous period was 145.5 and 65.5 million years ago. This is the last and final time period for dinosaurs. This is the time period when dinosaurs experience a mass extinction. This was the longest period of the Mesozoic era, it lasted 79 million years. Some popular dinosaurs from this time period include the Velociraptor, Giganotosaurus, Tyrannosaurus, and the Triceratops.

Velociraptor

Unlike in the Jurassic Park movies, the velociraptor was not in fact taller than a man, it was turkey sized. It walked on its hind legs, and had feathers.

Where found: Mongolia, China

Estimated length: 7 m (6.8 ft.)

Giganotosaurus

This dinosaur is at competition with the Spinosaurus for the position of ‘biggest land predator.’ Its skull was even bigger than the T-rex.

Where found: Argentina

Estimated length: 12-15 m (39-50 ft.)

Tyrannosaurus

One of the best known dinosaurs, it had what is estimated to be the greatest bite force of all land animals.

Where found: Western USA

Estimated length: 12 m (39 ft.)

Triceratops

This dinosaur had two large horns on top of its head and a third horn above its snout.

Where found: North America

Estimated length: 9 m (30 ft.)
